HMI Dashboard Overview

Modern ADAS systems communicate through visual cues on the instrument cluster. Familiarize yourself with these indicators before operating the vehicle.

1
Lane Keep Assist (LKA)
Green indicates active tracking. White indicates standby.
2
Adaptive Cruise (ACC)
Sets target speed and follow distance to the lead vehicle.
3
Blind Spot Monitor
Flashes yellow/amber when a vehicle is detected in your lateral zone.
1

LKA State

Monitors lane departure via camera.

2

Lead Vehicle Radar

Visual feedback for distance gap.

3

Proximity Alerts

Side-view mirror mirror alerts.

info

Key Insight: Multi-Modal Feedback

The HMI isn't just visual. When these icons flash red, the steering wheel may vibrate (haptic) or an audible chime will sound. In emergency braking scenarios, the icon will dominate the center display.
